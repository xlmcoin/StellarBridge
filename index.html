The Stellar-Powered
Bridge Infrastructure

StellarBridge leverages the Stellar Consensus Protocol — one of the most energy-efficient and battle-tested consensus mechanisms in crypto — to deliver cross-chain asset transfers that feel instantaneous.

Unlike legacy bridges relying on centralized validators, StellarBridge uses a decentralized federated agreement model, eliminating single points of failure while keeping costs microscopic for every user.

Trust & Safety

Security Is Not
an Afterthought

Every layer of StellarBridge is designed with adversarial conditions in mind. Our architecture assumes zero trust and verifies everything on-chain.

Third-Party Audits

All smart contracts and bridge logic are independently audited by leading blockchain security firms before any deployment.

Decentralized Validators

StellarBridge relies on a diverse, global set of quorum validators — no single entity can compromise the protocol.

Non-Custodial Design

Funds are never held by StellarBridge. Atomic swaps and escrow contracts ensure your assets remain under your control at all times.

Bug Bounty Program

We maintain an active bug bounty program rewarding responsible disclosure of any vulnerabilities found in our protocol.

Multi-Sig Treasury

Protocol funds are held in a 5-of-9 multi-signature treasury requiring consensus across internationally distributed keyholders.

Real-Time Monitoring

24/7 automated circuit-breakers and anomaly detection systems pause the bridge if suspicious activity is detected.
